Why It Works

Skiing Brings Them. Disco Carrot Makes Them Stay.

Modern ski guests choose resorts based on the full experience, not just conditions. Après ski, live music, and immersive atmosphere are now core drivers of satisfaction and repeat visits. Disco Carrot delivers all three in one package.

Revenue You Can Measure

When guests stay longer, they spend more. Disco Carrot creates a magnetic gathering point that keeps people on-site, rotating through your highest-margin outlets: bars, restaurants, retail, rentals, and lodging.

Memories That Drive Return Visits

Guests remember experiences, not infrastructure. Disco Carrot creates the kind of unique moment that becomes the story of the trip and the reason they book again next year.

Weather-Proof Programming

Snowfall is unpredictable. Culture and atmosphere are programmable. Disco Carrot helps protect your revenue when conditions are variable.

Safety First, Always

OSHA 10 and SPRAT certified operators. Fully insured. Safe volume levels. Integrated crowd control. Property protection built into every deployment. We answer the safety concerns before you have to ask.

Family Friendly

Disco Carrot is built for all ages. Genre-appropriate music and a visual spectacle that kids and adults enjoy equally. It's entertainment the whole mountain can experience.

Turnkey, Zero Hassle

DJ booking included. Generator capable or plug-in ready. Short deployment time from unload to mountain. We integrate into your operations without disruption and leave no trace.

Cost Effective.

Food and beverage is one of the highest-margin revenue categories at ski resorts. Après activations are among the most effective tools to increase participation in those streams. Disco Carrot extends guest dwell time, which translates directly to increased spend at your bars, restaurants, and retail.

What We Bring

Concert-Venue Quality Sound

The safest and easiest deployment of concert-venue quality sound, scalable for any size event. Designed for any location with safe volume levels that won't disrupt ongoing resort operations.

All-Terrain Vehicle

Safe and reliable transport platform, easily mobilized to virtually any location. Capable of traveling on snow, ice, dirt, gravel, pavement, and grass. Engineered to avoid damage to underlying surfaces.

Lighting & Visual Experience

Full club-style lighting and visual display included. Transforms any outdoor setting into an immersive event space, designed to complement and elevate the audio experience.

DJ Performances & MC

Genre-appropriate music programming tailored to your event. You book your own talent or we book for you. MC capability included for announcements, races, and awards.

Large-Scale Art Installation

The Disco Carrot snowcat jackalope is a visual spectacle that attracts crowds organically, functioning as both entertainment and landmark. The DJ booth provides protection for performers and equipment in all weather conditions.

Flexible Power

Generator capable or plug-in ready. Energy efficient operation. No external power hookup required, but we can use yours if available. We operate wherever the mountain takes us.

Safety, Insurance & Crowd Control

OSHA 10 and SPRAT certified. Fully insured. Integrated crowd control. Property protection. Safety lighting. Short deployment time from unload to mountain, and not disruptive to your ongoing operations.

Professional Content Capture

Every activation is captured with professional photography and video. Drone, GoPro, and video camera. You receive reusable marketing assets after every booking.
