What is Disco Carrot

Our Story

A Burning Man inspired idea born from the creative art community in Missoula, Montana. We took our two greatest passions, mountains and music, and built a one-of-a-kind mobile stage on a snowcat.

The result is a fully self-contained DJ stage, art installation, and sound system that travels anywhere the mountain takes it: snow, ice, dirt, gravel, pavement, or grass.
